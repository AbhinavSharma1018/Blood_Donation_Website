About Us

The purpose of the "Make a Blood Relation" website is to collect data about donors and seekers who are interested in donating blood or who require it. Anyone who wants to visit our website to donate blood can do so.Anyone who is in need of blood can visit our website and can fill details to receive blood In addition, this website makes it possible for any general consumer to request blood online. The admin is the primary authority if necessary and can perform addition, deletion, and modification. The main objective of the online "Make a Blood Relation" is to keep track of information about donors, blood banks, blood groups, and blood seekers.

Additionally Users can visit this website to get the information about the Guidelines for donating Blood and to recieve the Blood
